Understanding The War Compass Landscape

Strategic Orientation

Do you believe in diplomacy and norms, or power and deterrence?

Idealist: "Ceasefire and reform is the only solution."

Realist: "Only force changes behavior."

Moral Scope

Do you prioritize your own group, or value all lives equally?

Particularist: "Israel must protect its own first."

Universalist: "Palestinian and Israeli lives should count equally."

Strategic Ethics

Do you believe some actions are always wrong (e.g. targeting civilians), or that actions should be judged by their outcomes?

Deontological: "Israel bombing refugee camps is immoral, even if Hamas hides there."

Consequentialist: "If bombing shortens the war, it may be justified—even if civilians die."

Epistemic Trust

Do you trust leadership in war, or second-guess it?

Skeptic: "Leaders spin the truth. Independent observers see more clearly."

Deferent: "They have intel I don't. I trust they're doing what's best."
